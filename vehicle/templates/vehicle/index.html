About us

Banger & Co. is a small family owned vehicle hire company. We offer many varieties of vehicles ranging from small town cars to medium sized vans that you can book, but we don't allow customers under 25 years to book anything other than the small town cars. We allow the minimum booking period of 5 hours and a maximum of 2 weeks. No deposit is required to book with us! Just book now and pay on the pickup date. And yes, don't forget to bring a photocard of your driving license, 1 other form of identity from either a recent utility bill (within 3 months) or council tax statement, and the e-mail that you'll get once you book with us, on the pickup date.

How to book with us

  1. Select a booking time period.

  2. Select a vehicle from the list of serch results.

  3. Confirm your booking and click on Book button.
